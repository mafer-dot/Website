What is botany?

Botany is the scientific study of plants. It includes the study of their structure, properties, and biochemical processes. Botanists also study plant classification, the distribution of plants, and their relationships with the environment.

Benefits of botany

    Understanding Plants: Helps us learn how plants grow, reproduce, and survive.
    Medicine: Many medicines come from plants; botany helps discover new cures..
    Food Production: Improves farming techniques and crop yields to feed more people..
    Environment: Helps protect ecosystems and maintain biodiversity..
