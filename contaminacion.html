Pollution

Pollution is the contamination of the natural environment by harmful substances. It affects air, water, and soil, causing damage to health and ecosystems. Types of pollution include:

  • Air pollution: Harmful gases and particles in the atmosphere.
  • Water pollution: Contaminants entering rivers, lakes, and oceans.
  • Soil pollution: Chemicals and waste that degrade the land.
  • Plastic pollution: Accumulation of plastic waste harming wildlife.
