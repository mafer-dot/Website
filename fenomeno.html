Meteorological Phenomena

Meteorological phenomena are natural events that occur in the atmosphere, influencing weather and climate. Common phenomena include:

  • Rain: Water droplets falling from clouds.
  • Storms: Disturbances with strong winds, rain, thunder, and lightning.
  • Hurricanes: Large tropical storms with powerful winds.
  • Tornadoes: Violent rotating columns of air.
  • Droughts: Extended periods of low rainfall.
